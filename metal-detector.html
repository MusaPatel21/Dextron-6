Metal Detector

Our Metal Detector project focuses on providing high-precision security screening devices designed to identify metallic objects concealed on individuals or within luggage. These detectors ensure safety by quickly alerting security personnel about potential threats, making them essential for airports, corporate offices, courts, events, and other high-security environments.

Built using advanced detection coils and microprocessor-based technology, our metal detectors offer high accuracy, sensitivity adjustment, and minimal false alarms. Whether deployed as handheld scanners or walk-through gate systems, they are engineered for continuous use, easy operation, and fast inspection flow. Their durable construction allows them to function efficiently in both indoor and outdoor setups.

The Result Of Our Project

The implementation of our Metal Detector systems has improved security screening processes, reduced manual intervention, and enhanced public safety. Organizations now benefit from accurate threat detection, controlled entry procedures, and quicker movement of people through security checkpoints. This project delivers a reliable and indispensable solution for maintaining secure environments.

  • Accurate detection of concealed metallic objects in real time

  • Available in handheld and walk-through gate configurations

  • Adjustable sensitivity levels to suit diverse security needs

  • Ideal for airports, offices, events, public venues, and sensitive zones
