Barriers, Flap & Boom System

Our Barriers, Flap & Boom System project provides automated entrance management solutions designed to control and streamline vehicular and pedestrian access. Built with durable mechanical components and smart control logic, these systems ensure secure entry, prevent unauthorized movement, and improve traffic flow in high-demand areas such as parking lots, corporate campuses, gated communities, and industrial zones.

The system includes flap barriers for pedestrians and boom barriers for vehicles, both equipped with sensors, access readers, and intelligent controllers. These solutions operate seamlessly through RFID, QR codes, biometrics, or ticket-based authentication. Engineered for continuous operation, the system offers smooth movement, quick response time, and robust performance even under heavy traffic conditions.

The Result Of Our Project

The deployment of our Barriers, Flap & Boom System has significantly enhanced perimeter security, reduced manpower dependency, and improved operational efficiency. Organizations now experience regulated access, reduced tailgating, smooth traffic management, and accurate entry logs. This project delivers a dependable and automated access control solution built for modern security infrastructures.

  • Automated entry and exit control for pedestrians and vehicles

  • Supports RFID, biometric, QR code, and ticket-based authentication

  • High durability and quick operation for heavy-duty usage

  • Ideal for parking lots, corporate campuses, and gated facilities
