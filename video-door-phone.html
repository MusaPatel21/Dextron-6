Video Door Phone

Our Video Door Phone project provides a modern and secure visitor management solution that enables homeowners and businesses to visually verify and communicate with visitors before granting entry. The system combines video, audio, and door access control features to enhance safety, convenience, and control over building entrances.

Equipped with high-resolution cameras, clear audio communication, and optional remote unlocking features, the Video Door Phone allows users to interact with visitors through indoor monitors or mobile apps. Its durable outdoor panel withstands environmental conditions, while smart integration options make it compatible with access systems, CCTV, and alarms for a complete security setup.

The Result Of Our Project

With the implementation of our Video Door Phone system, properties now enjoy heightened security, better visitor screening, and improved entry management. Users have real-time awareness of who is at their door, reducing security risks and unauthorized access. This project delivers a smart, convenient, and user-friendly communication platform for secure building entry.

  • Visual and audio verification of visitors before granting access

  • Mobile app support for remote viewing and door control

  • Durable outdoor panel designed for all weather conditions

  • Ideal for homes, apartments, offices, and secure facilities
